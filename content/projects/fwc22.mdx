## How it works?

- The user mints their team supporter NFT.
- The $MATIC amount of NFTs minted goes into the prize pool.
- The NFTs' price, features, and images will evolve as the elimination rounds progress.
- After the FIFA World Cup 2022 concludes, the prize pool is divided among the owners of the NFTs from the winning team.
